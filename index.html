What people think of reading books

Books are important because books are full of ideas and experiences and people which, if you did not read them, you probably would never experience yourself in your entire life.

A book is a time machine, a magic mirror, and a wishing well. The experiences we enjoy through books change us — often for the better. Reading much like listening to music or doing exercise can be a form of meditation, as there are distractions everywhere from personal to professional life it’s hard to concentrate doing a single task, when we read a book all our attention is focused on the story and we get immersed in every detail that is mentioned in the book.

We have not yet found better ways to disseminate content or bring a wholesome enjoyment.

Our brain needs movement and by movement, it means we have to make ourselves busy in creative tasks and reading is the best workout for the mind, Reading makes us healthier and our mind sharp so find time from your busy schedule and read as much as you can. because the more knowledge we have the more equipped we will be to tackle upcoming challenges.
